About the Journey

Embark on a collective adventure along the breathtaking coastline of Béjaïa. This isn't just a boat ride; it's a shared exploration of hidden gems only accessible by sea. Join a diverse group of fellow travelers as we navigate the azure waters, exploring the mysterious caves of Les Aiguades and witnessing the majestic presence of Cap Carbon from its best vantage point. Our experienced local crew will share stories that bring the landscape to life, ensuring you leave with more than just photos.
